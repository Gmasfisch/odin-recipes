Pasta al pasto de la nonna

Description

Something you cant get tired of.

Ingredients

  • Fresh basil: 2 cups(packed)
  • Raw pine nuts: 1/3 cup
  • Lemon : 1/2 (juiced)
  • Garcil: 2 large cloves
  • Salt: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Extra virgin olive oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Water: 1/4 cup
  • Pasta (any type): 8-10 oz

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil and cook the pasta according to the package instructions
  2. While the pasta cooks, add all of the pesto ingredients except the olive oil to a food processor (or blender) nad pulse until the ingredients are finely chopped.
  3. Pour the olive oil and then water in a slow and steady stream into the food

    4. processor while the blades are still running and continue to pulse until it reaches your desired consistency.

    Pause to scrape down the sides if necessary. If too thick, add a splash of water with the blades still running to thin it out further.

  4. Once the pasta is cooked and drained, pour the pesto over top of the warm pasta and mix until fully combined. Serve inmediately
